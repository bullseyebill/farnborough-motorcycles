Welcome to Farnborough Motorcycles

We can help you what ever your motorbike or scooter needs from servicing and MoT's to fork seals, chains and sprockets or any other repair.

We are specialists for all major makes of motorcycle,including Ducati cam belts and servicing requirements. We can supply and fit aftermarket parts from most major brands including R&G, Scorpion, Viper, Motad and Akrapovic exhausts. We can also fit Hagon or K-Tec steering dampers, if you have an accessory in mind just ask!

If you live locally to our workshop we can collect and deliver your bike, just call us to discuss
